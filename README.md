# 💰 FlatFund

> Split rent, bills, and food expenses with your housemates — all in one place.

FlatFund is a web application that helps housemates manage shared living expenses. No more chasing people over WhatsApp or losing track of who owes what.

---

## 🏠 How It Works

Each household creates a shared **house group**. Housemates join using their email and password, and from there they can log every shared expense together — rent, utilities, and food.

---

## 💸 Expense Splitting

| Expense Type | Split Method |
|---|---|
| Rent | Equal split among all members |
| Bills | Equal split among all members |
| Food | Custom amount per person |

- Any member can log an expense and record who paid for it
- Rent and bills are automatically divided equally
- Food expenses allow flexible, custom amounts since not everyone eats the same

---

## 📊 Payment Tracking

- A clear dashboard shows the **current balance** for every member — who is owed and who owes
- Members can **manually mark debts as settled** after paying via cash or bank transfer
- Full **payment history** so nothing gets disputed

---

## 🔐 Authentication

- Secure **email + password** accounts
- Each user belongs to one household group
